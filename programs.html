Envisage Pre-incubation Program


Envisage Pre-incubation Program is a 12-week Program to nurture the young women minds to come up with an entrepreneurial mindset, to develop a market viable idea into a prototype in the Deep tech sector.
The first cohort of Envisage Pre-incubation Program was kickstarted with 16 ideas from G.Narayanamma Institute of Technology and Science (For Women). The students were given in-depth training on entrepreneurship and innovation starting from idea validation to creating business plans.
Outcome of the Program : Basic entrepreneurship awareness for students, Validating the idea and market, Product Market Fit, Business Model Canvas, Understanding of IP Rights and filing the IP, Working Prototype development.

Incubation program


Incubation Program is a 6 -month long program for women entrepreneurs in Deep tech sector with a working prototype. The Program offers a series of highly curated masterclass sessions on how to build a successful startup. Various aspects of marketing, finance, etc will be covered to launch the product to the market.
